# TalentHub AI Overview

TalentHub AI is a SaaS job platform for candidates, employers, and admins. It combines classic job-board workflows with AI assistance for resumes, matching, cover letters, candidate scoring, and employer analytics.

## Product Roles

- Candidate: manages profile, resume, applications, AI recommendations, chats, subscriptions.
- Employer: manages company, vacancies, pipeline, candidate scoring, analytics, subscriptions.
- Admin: moderates users, vacancies, payments, reports, content, permissions.

## Main Bounded Contexts

- Identity and access
- Candidate profiles and resumes
- Employer companies and vacancies
- Search and recommendations
- Applications and hiring pipeline
- Chat and notifications
- Billing and subscriptions
- AI services and usage limits
- Admin operations and audit logs
